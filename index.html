Thank you for participating, let's get started!

The following experiment consists of a short period of gameplay (3 minutes), followed by a short questionnaire (approximately 5-15 minutes).

Game instructions:
The goal of the game is to survive as long as possible by dodging the obstacles, the longer you survive the higher your score will be.  
Dodging is done by moving your character up and down, using the up and down arrow keys. On the top left of the screen you can see your 'HP', short for health points. This shows you how many hits you can take before you hit the game over screen and have to restart. You get infinite tries to beat your high score, until the three minute timer runs out. Upon which you will be redirected to a questionnaire, which we kindly ask you to fill in.      

Please play the game with your audio enabled, with nothing else playing in the background (no YouTube, Spotify, Radio, etc.).
Also make sure to play this on a computer, or on a tablet with a connected keyboard. The game does not work on phones.

Alright, that's all, once again, thank you for participating and more importantly, have fun!
